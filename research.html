Research Interests

We are interested in making machine learning and deep learning algorithms more secure, private, robust, efficient, and interpretable. Our research interests include federated learning, differential privacy, privacy attacks, and adversarial attacks (e.g., evasion and poisoning attacks). We focus on the practical applications and theoretical solutions based on statistical analysis. Our work directly benefits applications such as computer vision, natural language process, graph networks, and privacy-preserving machine learning systems. Here is my Google Scholar. Specifically, we are interested in the following topics:

  • Federated Learning
  • Differential Privacy
  • Adversarial Attacks
  • Privacy Attacks
  • Machine Learning Systems
